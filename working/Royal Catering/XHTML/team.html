Team

Chef Maher is born in Syria, Damascus city; well known as “Al – Sham “, or the Jasmine city – he made his way through cooking career from a very young age.

He discovered he had a passion for cooking, creating new recipes on his own, and especially using his talent and imagination more on the field of Pastry and bakery getting to be one of the much appreciated Pasty and Arabic Pastry Chef within the domain.

Maher started culinary skills in Damascus itself, and after rapidly growing and catching up with the cooking and baking – made his way out in Arabic region where he accumulated even more talent knowledge and appreciation.
