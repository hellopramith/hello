About Us

Full Catering Service & Event Planning

Royal Catering is a leader in providing excellent catering services in the country. It is our goal to provide our clients with exceptional food and incomparable service. Our reputation and growth have come from a result of our attention to detail, quality gourmet food, and friendly professional service. As a full service special event caterer, we will do what it is necessary to ensure our clients’ expectations are not only met, but exceeded.

Professional and devoted team. Our Event Planners are experts at organizing and executing any and all types of events. Be it large or small, we can provide any and all aspects. Everything is at your fingertips and completely customized for your individual needs.

Party Food Tray

Signature dishes to go. Clients can our signature dishes anytime they want, perfect for small gatherings and celebrations.

Convenience without the big budget. Have a party at home or in the office without the hassle of cooking for your guests and without the big budget of getting full catering service.

Sumptuous Cuisine. Enjoy the same signature dishes in our full catering service menu, from appetizers down to desserts.

Packed Meals

Individually packed meals perfect for large scale events where buffet or plated service is not possible. Served fresh, anywhere. Packed meals are prepared with the highest standards for quality to ensure that it is served delicious and fresh, where ever the event may be.

Gourmet food, delivered and ready to serve. The same signature dishes in our full catering service menu are available for packed meals. You can be confident that the best gourmet treats are being served in your event.

Event Styling

From and intimate dinner to extravagant corporate celebration, our goal is to make your event fit you like a glove, to expand your imagination and bring a fresh perspective to planning your event.

With Event Planners that have backgrounds in event management, wedding planning & design- Royal Catering can help you with all aesthetic aspects of an event or simply add the finishing touches to your celebration.

Mobile Bar

Fully mobile, Explosive Couch provides and creates unique concoctions, personalize drinks and to work coinciding with your event’s theme. Tailor-made drinks, drink requests, and themed drinks conceptualization are very much welcome. Surely one of the best way to make your guests stay, have fun and be merry throughout your event.
